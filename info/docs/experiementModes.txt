There are two basic modes: Basic and Full. 
Basic includes the minimum amount of functionality
Full includes all of the functionality availible 

The other modes: Test, Design, Auto, Parallel, Interactive etc. have all of the functionality availible in "Basic" and a specific subset of the functionality avaioble in "Full"
